Every Dog Deserves a Second Chance

At The Woofpack K9 Rescue, we’re more than just a charity—we’re a movement for pups who deserve love, care, and a forever home. Our mission is to rescue neglected dogs, rehabilitate them, and connect them with the families who will give them a happy life.

Support Our Mission

What We Do

We rescue dogs from unsafe environments and provide medical care, behavioral support, and a safe place to heal. Once they’re ready, we match them with loving families who understand the joy and responsibility of adoption.
